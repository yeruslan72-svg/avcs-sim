# AVCS Structural Integrity Module (SIM)

A diagnostic tool for measuring the structural health of decision-making systems in high-risk operations.

**Live demo:** [avcs-sim.streamlit.app](https://avcs-sim.streamlit.app)

---

## 🔍 What is SIM?

The Structural Integrity Module (SIM) evaluates decision architecture across five pillars:

| Pillar | What It Measures |
|--------|------------------|
| **Trigger Clarity** | Are escalation conditions mandatory or interpretive? |
| **Decision Ownership** | Is accountability singular and real-time? |
| **Protected Intervention** | Is stopping operations structurally safe? |
| **Override Transparency** | Are deviations visible and traceable? |
| **Drift Detection** | Is boundary movement tracked or ignored? |

Each pillar is scored 0–5. Total Structural Integrity Score: 0–25.

---

## 🧠 Why It Matters

Most systems don't fail because people are incompetent.  
They fail because structural weaknesses remain invisible until it's too late.

SIM makes the invisible visible — before failure, not after.

**Deepwater Horizon scored 3/25.**  
Not because of engineering failure, but because structural decision weaknesses were embedded long before the explosion.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Answer the diagnostic questions (5–10 minutes)
2. Get your Structural Integrity Score
3. Identify your weakest pillars
4. Download the report
5. Contact for a full in-depth audit
